Our process
This is how we do it
01

Meet & Greet

This is a time set aside for you to come and meet us at Bananas Creative and figure out if we are a match for you.

We will show you a snapshot of our portfolio, share our stories, and let you talk about your business.  It’s kind of like a first date, but you won’t need to plan an escape call!

02

Research

Digging Deep, we evaluate your current marketing approach - your print materials, website and social media to determine areas for growth.

Then we take a look a what some of your competitors are up to.   This information will be used to create your personalized branding and marketing strategy.

03

Creation of
your brand

This is the really fun part, the creative process.  You will be given some logo choices (basically the items we like so much we can’t decide!).

We will introduce you to the character that will represent your business to your audience (friendly, likeable, memorable) and show you what a service vehicle would look like with your branding.  We know that these vehicles are of vital importance to the brand.

04

Strategy Session
- Talk it Out

This is where we meet for a casual get together where we can talk about your goals for your business and discuss where the opportunities for growth are.

Then we provide you with some marketing strategies to really give your business a boost in your marketplace.  This includes website, social media, printed materials, and even ideas for your showroom or office space.

05

The Big Reveal

Your market area will be seeing your new look, new vehicle and new website for the first time.

Time to go out and be Fruitful.
